About us

One of the BIGGEST clothing canvas

We strive to make Clothing & Fashion a safe place and a wide canvas of creativity for teens because most are struggling with identity and knowing who they want to be or how they want to carry themselves.

So here is where we come in at. We use the highest quality products to make our clothes and we have world class designers to design to your needs.

The way we actually help is sparking the teens creativity to create their own designs at their own will, this will hopefully spark a connection to help them get to know themselves better creativity wise.

The reason we set out in Southern California is because that is where i lived for most of my life and i grew up around delienquents and i hate to see teens with potential wasting it all to look cool to others.
